Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Crystal Grimoire collects the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile picture (if using Google Sign-In)

Usage Data

  • Crystal identifications and collection data
  • AI consultation history
  • App usage analytics

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash reports (for improving stability)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the Crystal Grimoire service
  • Personalize your crystal recommendations and guidance
  • Process subscription payments
  • Send important service notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: Encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Firebase Authentication: Industry-standard security protocols
  • Google Cloud Storage: For uploaded images (encrypted)

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your data.

4. Third-Party Services

Crystal Grimoire uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and hosting
  • Google Gemini AI: Crystal identification and guidance
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • RevenueCat: Mobile subscription management

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing their use of your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your profile and collection data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Some anonymized usage data may be retained for analytics
  • Subscription records are retained as required by law

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your collection data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

7. Children's Privacy

Crystal Grimoire is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at:

Email: privacy@crystalgrimoire.com
Support: support@crystalgrimoire.com

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. Contact us to exercise these rights.
